Types of AR

Projection-based Augmented Reality (AR)

Projection-based AR systems projects the virtual image onto physical objects.

It can be interactive and project a digital keyboard on your desk, or a dialer on your hand.

It might be non-interactive and it can be used to create projection of objects that you can position and see in depth.
