Types of AR

Outlining-based Augmented Reality (AR)

Outlining-based AR application merges the outline of any object, so that users can pick up the same with their hands.

Users would also be able to manipulate it with a virtual object that does not exist in real view.

The concept of this AR is to track the outline of the person with the help of a camera and then reorient the virtual object.
