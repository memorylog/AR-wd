AR vs VR

Augmented Reality


Augmented Reality (AR) enchances Real life by superimposing virtual images and adds graphics, sound to the Real world.




User maintains a sense of presence in the Real world.

They can interact with the Real world and is not cut off from Real world.




Augmented Reality (AR) makes use of smartphones.


Virtual Reality


Virtual Reality (VR) replaces the Real world with Virtual world.





User can't see the Real world around them.

They are completely immersed into virtual world and cut off from Real world.




Virtual Reality (VR) makes use of Head Mounted Displays (HMD).
