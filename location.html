Types of AR

Location-based Augmented Reality (AR)

Location-based AR in most cases is used for AR location browsers which help users discover interesting places within their current location.

This method works by detecting the user’s location and orientation by reading data from the mobile’s GPS, digital compass and accelerometer and predicting where the user is looking;

Then related information is added on screen about the objects that can be seen from the camera.
