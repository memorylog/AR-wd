Types of AR

Marker-based Augmented Reality (AR)

Marker-based AR, also known as Recognition-based AR, uses a camera and some type of visual marker, such as a QR/2D code, to produce a result only when the marker is sensed by a reader.

Distinct, but simple patterns (such as a QR code) are used as the markers, because they can be easily recognized and do not require a lot of processing power to read.

The position and orientation is also calculated, in which some type of content and/or information is then overlaid on the marker.
